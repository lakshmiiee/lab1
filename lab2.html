IPL 2025 Final | 18-year wait ends as RCB lift maiden IPL trophy

After 18 years of heartbreak, Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) have finally clinched their first-ever IPL title in 2025. The team, led by Rajat Patidar and with key contributions from Virat Kohli, defeated Punjab Kings in a thrilling final.
